Dataset:  hairlength.dat

Source: C. Robbins and M.G. Robbins (2003). "Scalp hair Length. I. Hair
Length in Florida Theme Parks: An Approximation of Hair Length in
the United states of America," Journal of Cosmetic Science, Vol. 54,
pp. 53-62

Description: Classification of a sample of 2400 US women by Hair Length and 
Age:
Hair Length: 1=Short, 2=Medium, 3=Long
Age:  1=14-24, 2=25-34, 3=35-49, 4=50-60

Variables/Columns
Age     8
Hair Length    16
Count       20-24
